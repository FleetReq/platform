§ 2.0 — MODULE SPECIFICATIONS
Core modules
Six integrated modules covering the full lifecycle of fleet operation and compliance.
MOD-01 · FUEL

Fuel Tracking

Fill-up logging with auto-calculated MPG, cost-per-mile, and fleet-wide fuel trend analysis.

MOD-02 · MAINT

Maintenance Records

Service history with mileage + time-based interval engine. Proactive alerts before overdue status.

MOD-03 · ANALYTICS

Performance Analytics

Visual dashboards for cost tracking, efficiency trends, and cross-vehicle comparisons.

MOD-04 · ACCESS

Access Management

Role-based permissions: Owner / Editor / Viewer. Multi-org support with org-scoped data isolation.

MOD-05 · MOBILE

PWA Deployment

Progressive web app with offline capability. Home screen install, background sync, service worker caching.

MOD-06 · EXPORT

Report Generation

CSV, PDF, JSON output. IRS-compliant mileage logs. Business and per-vehicle breakdowns.
